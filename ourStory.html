Our Story

It is fall of 2018, leaves and love are in the air and so were we. We met through our adoration of music in college. Natalie was infatuated with Caleb's musical abilities, and perhaps the looks too. One day, she mentioned to her Matron of Honor, Brandi, that she thought he was cute. Brandi ran with that information and played the ultimate matchmaker. The rest is history!

Natalie and Caleb dated throughout college and moved to Tennessee. What an adventure Tennessee was! Moving to another state away from friends and family was challenging, but they had each other to lean on.
